Project: ELO Tracker
Name: Relo?
Description: Platform that lets users create groups/organizations for an activity and compete with each other. 
    Tracking player elo, match history, and rankings. Example: Groups of friends who play ping pong together 
    can track their mathces and establish a ranking between them. 

Needs: Rankings in competition only exists in formal sports leagues, and competition metrics,
    such as elo, only exists in specific solo sports, chess, or online gaming. However friend groups 
    and informal organizations are competing in various activities - such as table tennis, super smash bros,
    JiuJitsu - and competitors argue about who is the best. While the light-hearted arguing
    is a fun aspect of the competition, a way for these friend groups to keep track of their competition would 
    keep them motivated to keep competing against each other. A give them a chance to grow their
    competition outside the friend group and incorporate others.

MVP: 
    Requirements:
        1. users can create activity groups and invite other users to join
        2. Activity groups track matches between users for activity
        3. Activity groups create an elo for each user participating within the group 

    Non-Functional Requirements:
        - Mobile and Website
            Create website first that you can test at work
        - Secure
        - Internal monitoring and tracking
